PointBank Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how PointBank ("the Bot") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when used within Discord servers. By using the Bot, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information Collected

PointBank collects only the minimum data necessary to provide its functionality. This includes:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server (Guild) IDs
  • Point balances and related economy data

The Bot does not collect or store email addresses, passwords, payment information, IP addresses, or direct message content.

3. How Information Is Used

The collected information is used exclusively to:

  • Track virtual point balances
  • Operate economy-related features
  • Provide leaderboard and reward functionality
  • Maintain service stability and performance

No data is used for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on a private server controlled by the Bot administrator. Reasonable technical and administrative safeguards are implemented to protect stored information from unauthorized access or misuse.

5. Data Sharing

PointBank does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share user data with third parties. Data remains confined to the operation of the Bot within Discord servers where it is installed.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for the continued operation of the Bot. Server administrators may request removal of server-related data. If the Bot is removed from a server, associated data may be deleted at the administrator’s discretion.

7. User Rights

Server administrators may contact the Bot owner to request clarification regarding stored data or request removal of data associated with their server.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect operational, technical, or legal changes. Updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact the Bot administrator through the official Discord server or other designated communication channels.
